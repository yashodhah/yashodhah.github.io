About me.

My passion for computer science was ignited by the excitement of solving complex mathematical problems through programming. It all started back in early 2012 when I stumbled upon Project Euler, a captivating series of challenges that blended math and coding.

Since then, I've fully immersed myself in the world of programming, with Java, Python, and JavaScript becoming my primary languages of choice. Recently, I've been eagerly adding Rust to my skill set, as I believe in constantly expanding my knowledge and staying up-to-date with new technologies.

For me, the specific programming language is not a limitation. Instead, I see it as an opportunity to leverage my existing knowledge and skills and apply them to learning new languages and technologies. I believe that the fundamental principles and problem-solving approaches in programming can be mapped across different languages. This mindset has served me well so far, allowing me to quickly adapt and grasp new languages and frameworks

Throughout my journey, adaptability has been crucial to my success. Whether in academic or industry settings, I have embraced a mindset of continuous learning, constantly absorbing new insights and applying them to real-world problems. I understand that there is an abundance of knowledge yet to be discovered, and I'm motivated to keep pushing the boundaries of what I can achieve.
